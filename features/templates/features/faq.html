Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating, fund usage, and more.

How are the funds used?
All funds are used for approved village projects. Every expense is recorded and visible on the Fund Usage page, along with bills and photos.
How can I donate?
Click the "Donate" button on any project. You must be logged in to donate. Choose your amount and payment method, and you'll receive a receipt after donating.
How can I track my donation?
Log in and visit your profile to see your donation history. All donations are also listed (with your name or as anonymous) on the Fund Usage page.
How does voting for fund usage work?
If enabled, villagers can log in and vote on how funds should be used for upcoming projects. Results are shown in real-time for transparency.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes! Just check the "Donate anonymously" option on the donation form. Your name will not be shown publicly.
